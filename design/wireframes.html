龙虾Lóngxiā - a Chinese study app

Reading, writing & speaking on the HSK 3.0 spine - with a real notebook, interactive 田字格 practice, and a red-pen AI that annotates exactly what you point at.

01 · Design language not another card-and-gradient app

Palette - paper, ink, one red pen

The motif - 田字格

The practice grid is the app's structural signature - background of every writing surface, the app mark, the loading state. Unmistakably Chinese handwriting; impossible to mistake for a generic SaaS template.

What we're deliberately avoiding

  • Avoid Purple→blue gradient hero, glassmorphism, drop-shadow card soup
  • Avoid Inter/Space-Grotesk default, emoji section headers, everything centered
  • Avoid The cream + serif + terracotta "AI editorial" cliché
  • Prefer Ruled-notebook layout, editorial column, semantic color only
